In Loving Memory of My Mother

Your love remains a gentle light in every quiet day, guiding me with grace, patience, and warmth.

In silence you left, yet in my heart you remain.
A light no darkness can dim,
a love that time cannot steal.

Calculating days...

Hope is the thing with feathers.
- Emily Dickinson

Love is the bridge between memory and forever.
